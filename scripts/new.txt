Bananas are berries but strawberries aren't. Botanically speaking, strawberries are aggregate fruits, while bananas meet the criteria for being classified as berries.
Sharks existed before trees. Sharks have been around for over 400 million years, whereas trees appeared around 350 million years ago.
Octopuses have three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.
Bees can recognize human faces. They use a technique called "configural processing" to remember patterns in faces.
Water can boil and freeze at the same time under certain conditions, known as the "triple point," where temperature and pressure cause the three phases of matter (solid, liquid, and gas) to coexist.
Koalas sleep up to 22 hours a day. This is due to their low-energy diet of eucalyptus leaves.
The Eiffel Tower can grow more than 6 inches in the summer because metal expands in the heat.
Humans share 60% of their DNA with bananas. This highlights how interconnected all life on Earth is at a molecular level.
Cows have best friends and can become stressed when they are separated from them.
Sloths can take up to a month to digest food. Their metabolism is incredibly slow compared to other animals.
Wombat poop is cube-shaped. This helps it stay in place and mark territory without rolling away.

Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

A day on Venus is longer than its year. Venus takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

Sea otters hold hands when they sleep. This prevents them from drifting apart in the water.

Butterflies taste with their feet. They have taste sensors on their legs to help them locate food.

The human nose can detect over 1 trillion different scents. Though it was once thought to be limited, recent research shows the nose is far more sensitive than previously believed.

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all the Earth's beaches. Scientists estimate there are around 100 billion galaxies in the observable universe.

An octopus can regrow a lost arm. It takes just a few months for a new, fully functioning arm to regenerate.

Goats have rectangular pupils. This allows them to see 320 degrees around them, giving them a wide field of vision to spot predators.

A single strand of spider silk is five times stronger than steel of the same diameter. Despite its strength, it's incredibly lightweight and flexible.
